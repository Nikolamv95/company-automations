## Voice & Tone

- **Voice:** Strong, with weight — premium but human. Clear and honest. Confident without excessive hype. Disciplined, not chaotic. *(Required)*
- **Tone:** Respectful, direct, human, confident, clear — like a brand with a standard, not a market stall vendor.
- **POV:** We do not write in 1st person singular as Hristo Stoichkov without approval. When the brand speaks — it speaks as an organization with values.
- **Reading level:** 5th grade. Short sentences. One idea per paragraph.

**We write like this (YES):**
- "A brand with a standard, not empty promises."
- "Sign up for early access on March 15."
- "Clear approach. Clear start. Clear communication."
- "Built for people who value discipline and consistency."

**We do NOT write like this:**
- "The most brutal product on the market!"
- "It will change your life in days!"
- "We're going to blow it up!"
- "The sickest thing you've ever seen!!!"
- "Guaranteed results from day one!"
- CAPS LOCK for drama
- Too many exclamation marks
- "miracle", "guaranteed", "100% effect" — without justification
- Pseudo-scientific phrases
- Jargon

**Style rules:**
- 1 idea = 1 sentence/paragraph
- Strong, short sentences
- Measured, controlled emotion
- Clear CTAs
- Consistent terminology: "March 15 — early access", "April 1 — official launch"

**Before publishing — mandatory check:**
- Does it sound like Hristo Stoichkov's premium brand?
- Is it clear to the user?
- Is it misleading?
- Does it have unnecessary cheap hype?
- Does it make risky promises?
- Does it speak respectfully to the audience?
- Does it sound like Stoitchkov Nutrition, not a generic ad?
- Does it comply with all restrictions?

- **Primary demographic:** Active people with a busy lifestyle — athletes, people with intensive work schedules, older adults looking for a stable routine.

## Compliance Notes

- Products are dietary supplements — not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent diseases.
- For medically sensitive questions → use the safe route response (see below).
- We do not give personal dosage recommendations in chat or comments.
- For pregnancy, breastfeeding, people under 18, medication use, or chronic illness → refer to a doctor/specialist.

**Safe Route responses (mandatory in these situations — do not modify):**

- *Specific disease/diagnosis:* "Stoitchkov Nutrition products are dietary supplements — not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent diseases. For a specific condition, the decision is made with a doctor."
- *Combination with medication:* "If you are taking medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist before adding any new supplement."
- *Pregnancy/breastfeeding:* "During pregnancy or breastfeeding — consult a doctor before any supplement."
- *Children/teenagers:* "Products are for adults and children over 10. For minors — the decision is made with a doctor or pediatrician."
- *Chronic illness:* "For chronic illness we do not make general recommendations. Consult a doctor."

## Influencer Communication

### Tone & Rules

**We do (YES):**
- Address personally by name
- Show that we know who the person is
- Say why we're reaching out — logically, not randomly
- Briefly explain the brand concept
- Give a clear proposal (gift / collab / story / reel / event)
- Leave space for their natural style

**We do NOT:**
- Generic "Hello, we want advertising"
- Overly long messages
- Pressure for a quick response
- Overly controlling instructions in the first message
- Medical claims or aggressive promises in the brief

### Template: First message

```
Hi [Name],

We're reaching out on behalf of Mr. Hristo Stoichkov and his new brand.

We are currently preparing a launch campaign and would love to have your support in this new venture.
We would like to send you a gift on his behalf, from the first batch of our products, and if possible, to support us with a video/photo that we can publish on the official website and our profiles.

Best regards,
The Hristo Stoichkov Team — Stoitchkov Nutrition
```

### Template: Follow-up (when interest is shown)

```
Hi [Name],
Thank you for your response.

We're sending you a brief summary of our idea:

- [Product] — [one-line description]
- Direction: natural content with a product recommendation
- Focus: quality, trust, and a recommendation to your audience that it's worth trying

What matters to us is that the content is in your style and feels natural to your audience.

If you confirm, we'll prepare the shipment and additional information.
Thank you for your support!
```

### What we want from influencers

**We want:** Natural communication. Personal impression. Authenticity. A respectful and clean tone.

**We do NOT want:** Medical claims. Guarantees. Overplayed advertising phrases. Copy-paste identical content from everyone.

### We do NOT write to influencers (prohibited phrases)

- "We have a super powerful product that guarantees results."
- "We want 3 stories by tomorrow."
- "Say it's the best on the market."
- "Do exactly this text 1:1."
- "It's going to explode, we'll destroy the market."

## Brand Pillars & Positioning

1. **The name speaks for itself.** Hristo Stoichkov is recognizable worldwide without explanation. They see it and they already know.

2. **Cause — 1% of revenue to charity** toward a large organization with global trust.
   ⚠️ This is NOT used as a marketing trick. It is not aggressively highlighted in advertising materials.

3. **Subscription-first, but not a trap.** The model is oriented toward subscription, but people can also order one-time. We don't push toward subscription — we offer it as the logical choice.

4. **Three products = complete coverage.** The goal is for Daily Pack 94 + Water Goal 6 + Magne Master 8 to cover all the needs of one person — regardless of their lifestyle.

5. **Positioning:** Well-selected ingredients, necessary for young and old for a better life. Not "sports supplement." Not "for advanced." For everyone.
