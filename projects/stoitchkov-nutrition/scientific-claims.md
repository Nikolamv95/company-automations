## Daily Pack 94

### B-Vitamins (B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, B12)

- Function as essential cofactors in mitochondrial energy metabolism and neurotransmitter synthesis
- Meta-analyses (2000+ participants) confirm B-complex reduces subjective stress levels and improves sleep quality
- B-complex has positive effect on mood in healthy individuals and those at risk, improving resilience under psycho-emotional load
- **Vitamin B12 (cobalamin):** Essential for neuronal stability, synaptic plasticity, and neuroinflammation regulation via the gut-brain axis (GBA)
- B12 deficiency → homocysteine accumulation → linked to affective disorders and emotional dysregulation
- Adequate B12 associated with lower risk of depression and behavioral issues

### Myo-Inositol (Vitamin B8)

- Acts as secondary messenger in signaling pathways of insulin, TSH, FSH
- Precursor of inositol triphosphate in cell membranes → regulates intracellular calcium and GLUT4 glucose transporter translocation
- Clinical studies: supplementation improves hormonal and metabolic profile in patients with insulin resistance and metabolic syndrome
- Meta-analyses confirm significant reduction in fasting insulin levels and HOMA index
- Also supports nervous system function under prolonged mental load

### Vitamins D3 + K2 (Synergy)

- D3 stimulates synthesis of calcium-binding proteins (osteocalcin, matrix Gla protein / MGP) — but these are produced in inactive form
- K2 is the required cofactor for activation via gamma-carboxylation
- Activated osteocalcin directs calcium to bone matrix → increases mineral density
- Activated MGP actively inhibits calcification of soft tissue and vascular walls
- Clinical reviews confirm this combination provides higher safety and efficacy for skeletal and cardiovascular health vs. either vitamin alone
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "D3 + K2 — a synergistic pair for healthy bones and the cardiovascular system."

### Vitamin E + Selenium (Antioxidant Network)

- Vitamin E (tocopherol): primary fat-soluble antioxidant, protects cell membranes from lipid peroxidation
- Studies in elderly show improved immune response and reduced risk of respiratory infections
- Selenium: component of glutathione peroxidase (GPx) — enzyme that neutralizes free radicals from cellular respiration and hormone synthesis
- In patients with autoimmune thyroiditis: selenium reduces thyroid inflammation and thyroid antibody levels
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "Vitamin E + Selenium — antioxidant protection."

### Siberian Ginseng (Eleutherococcus senticosus)

- Classified as adaptogen — increases general non-specific resistance to stress
- Acts as actoprotector: increases physical and mental performance WITHOUT increasing oxygen consumption or causing thermal exhaustion
- Studies confirm improved cognitive function, attention, and endurance in healthy individuals and patients with chronic fatigue
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "Siberian ginseng — a natural adaptogen for physical and mental endurance."

## Water Goal 6

### Electrolyte Balance Physiology

- During physical activity or stress, the body loses not just water but critical electrolytes: Sodium (Na), Potassium (K), Magnesium (Mg), Calcium (Ca)
- Electrolyte deficiency impairs osmotic pressure regulation, nerve impulse transmission, and muscle contraction
- Water Goal 6 formula provides: Electrolyte blend (Na, Mg, Ca, Cl) 1000mg + Potassium 100mg + Calcium 195mg (24% NRV) + Magnesium 125mg (33% NRV)

### Taurine (500mg)

- Amino acid that supports muscle function, reduces oxidative stress, improves endurance during physical activity
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "Taurine supports muscle function and reduces oxidative stress."

### Rhaponticum carthamoides / Levzea Extract (50mg)

- Adaptogenic herb with long history in sports medicine
- Contains phytoecdysteroids (ecdysterone) — compounds structurally similar to insect hormones, but with anabolic-like effects in mammals WITHOUT hormonal side effects
- Studies show: improved physical endurance, reduced fatigue, supports muscle recovery
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "Levzea supports physical endurance and muscle recovery."

### Vitamin C (100mg / 125% NRV)

- Essential for collagen synthesis, immune function, and as water-soluble antioxidant
- Supports adrenal function under stress

## Magne Master 8

### Three Forms of Magnesium (Rationale)

- **Magnesium citrate:** Fast absorption, supports muscle relaxation
- **Magnesium bisglycinate:** Gentle on stomach, most effective for improving sleep quality
- **Magnesium taurate:** Supports nervous system and cardiovascular health
- Combination of three forms provides broader, more complete coverage than any single magnesium form

### Amino Acids

- **Glycine (1000mg):** Associated with better sleep quality and muscle health; inhibitory neurotransmitter that promotes relaxation
- **L-Glutamine (1000mg):** Supports gut health and immune response, especially in a stressed organism; most abundant amino acid in muscle tissue
- **Citrulline DL-malate (1000mg):** Reduces post-exercise muscle soreness; precursor to arginine → nitric oxide pathway → improved blood flow and recovery

### Vitamin B6 (1.5mg)

- Participates in synthesis of GABA — the main inhibitory neurotransmitter of the brain
- Direct synergy with magnesium for stress management and sleep quality
- **Consumer-facing claim (approved):** "Vitamin B6 participates in the synthesis of GABA — the main calming neurotransmitter."

### Zinc Citrate (10mg)

- Supports immune function, hormonal balance, skin health
- Zinc deficiency linked to sleep disturbances and impaired recovery

## Safe Claim Boundaries

**ALLOWED (structure/function claims):**
- "Supports normal muscle function"
- "Contributes to reducing fatigue"
- "Supports normal functioning of the nervous system"
- "Contributes to maintaining normal bones"

**NOT ALLOWED (disease claims):**
- "Cures", "treats", "prevents", "reduces the risk of disease X"
- Specific percentage efficacy claims without citation in consumer copy
- Any claim implying the product replaces medical treatment
