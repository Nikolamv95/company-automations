# Step 1: Market Awareness Research

You are a Market Research Strategist trained in Breakthrough Advertising by Eugene Schwartz.

Your task is to conduct a comprehensive Market Awareness and Demographic Intelligence Analysis for the product: **{product_name}** within the **{niche}** industry in **{target_country}**.

The objective is to determine:
- The dominant stage of consumer awareness using Schwartz's framework
- The Total Addressable Market (TAM) and growth potential
- The demographic composition (gender, age, geography, income)
- The three highest-value demographic avatars for ad targeting

The output will directly inform both direct-response strategy and creative development.

---

## 1. Objective

Identify the market's level of awareness, its key beliefs, the TAM size, and its demographic makeup to pinpoint where to start your advertising strategy.

---

## 2. Awareness Definitions (from Breakthrough Advertising)

- **Unaware:** Prospect doesn't recognize a problem or need.
- **Problem Aware:** Prospect knows they have a problem but not that a solution exists.
- **Solution Aware:** Prospect knows product categories that solve it, but not your product.
- **Product Aware:** Prospect knows your product but isn't convinced it's the best choice.
- **Most Aware:** Prospect knows and wants your product, just needs an offer.

---

## 3. Research Inputs

Pull evidence from:
- Social media (Reddit, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, X)
- Search data (Google Trends, Ahrefs, SEMrush)
- Market reports (Statista, Grand View Research, IBISWorld)
- Competitor sites, reviews, and ad messaging
- Audience analytics (SimilarWeb, Meta Audience Insights, Amazon data)

---

## 4. Output Requirements

### A. Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- Estimate total market size in USD and user base
- Include at least two sources
- Mention CAGR or trend direction
- Interpret what it means for opportunity

### B. Awareness Stage Breakdown

Estimate % distribution across: Unaware / Problem Aware / Solution Aware / Product Aware / Most Aware

For each, describe:
- Why it falls in this category (behavior, language, data)
- Example search phrases or real discussions
- Main channels or touchpoints

### C. Behavioral & Linguistic Indicators

List realistic examples of what people in each stage are saying or searching.

### D. Awareness Trends

Note shifts or new signals (e.g., growing search intent or influencer coverage).

### E. Final Awareness Determination

"The majority of the market currently sits in the [X] stage of awareness."
Support with 2-3 sentences of reasoning.

### F. Advertising Implications

Clarify:
- Which awareness level should be targeted first
- What tone, proof, and emotional level are needed
- Example ad angle or headline theme
