## Your Task

Go through these research docs in detail to create ONE master research document that contains ALL of the most critical information needed to successfully sell this product with Facebook Ads.

This document will be used to give LLMs information to write copy and create marketing content focusing on direct-response sales copy, advertorials, and ad scripts.

The purpose is so that we don't have to upload the original 3 docs every single time we want to create new marketing material.

This document will be used in perpetuity to prompt LLMs to write effective marketing materials for selling this product.

**I'm not looking for:** specific information on market size or any specific strategies/tactics from the research docs.

**I am looking for:**

---

## 1. Target Market Demographic Overview

Focus on people who fall into the **{awareness_level}** market segments primarily.

---

## 2. Target Market Psychographic Overview

**What are their problems and pain points?**
How do they describe these things IN THEIR OWN WORDS?

[Pull verbatim language from the avatar research doc]

**What are their hopes and dreams?**
Specifically, what does their ideal future state look like where their problem has been solved? What would that look like? How would they look and feel? When they dream and imagine this future, what else is true about their life at this point?
How do they describe these things IN THEIR OWN WORDS?

**How do they view themselves more broadly, what language should we use when speaking to them, and what language should we avoid?**

**What are the Primary Promises we can make to them** — that they would want to hear — when it comes to solving their pain points?

---

## 3. Biggest Objections to Our Marketing Campaigns

What will their biggest objections be to our marketing campaigns for this product, and how can we address those?

---

## 4. Existing Solutions & Their Failures

What existing solutions has the market tried already? Why are those existing solutions not adequate enough?

## Additional Output: product-marketing-context.md

After creating the master document, also generate a condensed version (max 500 words) saved as `output/{product}/product-marketing-context.md`.

This condensed version is loaded by all copy-writing agents as shared brand context. Include:
- Product name, price point, primary channel
- Target audience (1-2 sentences)
- Primary desire to lead all copy with
- Tone and voice guidelines
- Top 3-5 claims/benefits
- What NOT to say (language to avoid)
- Key proof points available

---

## Formatting Notes

- Keep the master document dense and factual, not fluffy.
- Use the customer's exact language throughout, not polished marketing speak.
- Reading level: 5th grade equivalent — this is used to prompt other LLMs so clarity matters.
- Organize with clear section headers.
