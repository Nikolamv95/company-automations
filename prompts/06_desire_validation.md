# Step 6: Mass Desire Validation

I am doing deep research on mass desires and validating them in the market for a product in the **{niche}** space. The specific product is **{product_name}**.

I would like for you to dive deep to help me understand which of the candidate mass desires are truly held by the market in **{target_country}**, and how strong they are compared to each other.

I have used an LLM to extract the mass desires from my product. That output is in: `output/{product}/05_desire_extraction.md`

---

## Instructions (follow in this exact order)

**1.** Restate the potential mass desires clearly as short statements (pull from `05_desire_extraction.md`).

**2.** For each desire, research evidence from real customer language:
   - Look at Amazon reviews, Reddit threads, public forums, Quora answers, YouTube comments, or social media discussions related to the problem/product category.
   - Pull out verbatim quotes, stories, or complaints that reveal emotional pain, frustration, or urgency connected to that desire.
   - Summarize the key emotional triggers customers express in their own words.

**3.** Map each desire back to Schwartz's 3 dimensions using the research:
   - **Urgency:** Based on the language used, how immediately do people want this solved?
   - **Staying Power:** Does this problem or desire repeat daily/chronically, or does it fade?
   - **Scope:** How many people appear to share this desire (broad vs niche)?

   Give each dimension a 1-5 score, with reasoning.

**4.** Rank the desires from strongest to weakest based on total score + evidence of emotional intensity.

**5.** Highlight **ad implications:** For the top 2-3 desires, explain why they are the most scalable for advertising and how they can be dramatized (visuals, emotions, storylines).

**6.** Note **supporting desires:** Show how the lower-ranked desires can still reinforce or be layered under the dominant ones in ad creative.

---

## Final Output

At the end, of however many mass desires were provided in step 5, rank and choose the **top 5 most prominent mass desires** to build a marketing campaign around.

These should be the 5 you believe are most likely to succeed based on the research.

Rank them as **"I want..." statements**.

---

## Formatting Notes

- Cite every quote with platform and approximate date.
- Keep tone analytical and evidence-based.
- Avoid em dashes; use commas or periods.
- Do not include desires that don't have clear market evidence.
