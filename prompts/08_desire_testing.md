## Story Format: Personal Discovery Story

Tell a raw, personal story of desperate discovery that transforms how the reader sees their problem. Make them feel like you're sharing something you discovered at 2 AM that changed everything.

**The story you're telling:** Someone in crisis discovers the REAL reason behind their problem (that nobody talks about) and finds hope in understanding what's actually happening. This isn't ad copy — it's someone sharing a desperate discovery that saved something precious.

---

## Structure

### OPENING (max 20 words)
Create an irresistible hook that makes them NEED to know what happens next. Use a conversation, observation, or moment that's so specific and intriguing they can't stop reading.

### LET THE STORY FLOW NATURALLY

**Vulnerability — why this matters so deeply**
- Show emotional stakes through specific daily moments
- Build to breaking point

**The desperate search**
- Late-night googling, useless advice, timestamps
- Failed attempts with common solutions

**THE DISCOVERY (choose what fits your story — do NOT default to forum/Reddit posts)**

Instead, consider:
- A doctor/expert who finally explains what's really happening
- Your own observation that suddenly makes everything click
- A book/study that contradicts everything you believed
- Someone else's casual comment that changes your perspective
- A test result that reveals the hidden issue
- Trying something different out of desperation and noticing unexpected results

The unique mechanism (problem and solution) emerges through YOUR realization, not through quoted conversations.

**The transformation**
- Show skepticism → hope → small changes → transformation
- Specific sensory details

**Circle back**
- What this means in time, moments, experiences you would have lost

**Soft close**
- End with soft urgency — sharing crucial information, not selling
- Single CTA

---

## Writing Rules

- 5th grade reading level throughout
- Use the avatar's exact language from `06_desire_validation.md` and `03_avatar.md`
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- No superlatives without proof
- The product mechanism is revealed mid-story, not at the beginning
- Product name appears only after the discovery moment
- CTA: single clear action at the end

---

## Output

Write 1 complete ad (1200-1400 words).

After the ad, add a 2-sentence note explaining which desire from the validation doc this copy is testing and why this story structure fits that desire.
