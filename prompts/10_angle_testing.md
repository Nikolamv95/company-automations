## Story Format: Personal Discovery Story

Tell a raw, personal story of desperate discovery that transforms how the reader sees their problem. Make them feel like you're sharing something you discovered at 2 AM that changed everything.

**The story you're telling:** Someone in crisis discovers the REAL reason behind their problem (that nobody talks about) and finds hope in understanding what's actually happening. This isn't ad copy — it's someone sharing a desperate discovery that saved something precious.

---

## Structure

### OPENING (max 20 words)
An irresistible hook that makes them NEED to know what happens next. Use a conversation, observation, or moment that's so specific and intriguing they can't stop reading.

### LET THE STORY FLOW NATURALLY

**Vulnerability**
- Why this matters so deeply
- Show emotional stakes through specific daily moments
- Build to breaking point

**The desperate search**
- Late-night googling, useless advice, timestamps
- Failed attempts with common solutions

**THE DISCOVERY (choose what fits — do NOT default to forum/Reddit posts)**
- A doctor/expert who finally explains what's really happening
- Your own observation that suddenly makes everything click
- A book/study that contradicts everything you believed
- Someone else's casual comment that changes your perspective
- A test result that reveals the hidden issue
- Trying something different out of desperation and noticing unexpected results

The unique mechanism (problem and solution) emerges through YOUR realization, not through quoted conversations.

**The transformation**
- Skepticism → hope → small changes → transformation
- Specific sensory details

**Circle back**
- What this means in time, moments, experiences you would have lost

**Soft close**
- End with soft urgency — sharing crucial information, not selling
- Single CTA

---

## The Unique Mechanisms (woven naturally)

- **Hidden Problem:** The REAL reason for failure that explains why obvious solutions don't work
- **Real Solution:** Why this approach works when others fail, connecting directly to the hidden problem

---

## Writing Style

- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Natural line breaks
- Specific details (times, names, numbers)
- Physical reactions
- Conversational tone
- NO forum-style quoted blocks unless absolutely essential to your specific story

---

## The Feeling to Create

"This person went through exactly what I'm going through. They found something I need to know. This explains why I've been failing."

---

## Avoid

- Defaulting to Reddit/forum format
- Long quoted sections
- Structured sections with headers
- Explaining mechanisms directly
- Marketing speak

---

## Note on Angle vs Desire Testing

This is ANGLE testing — each of the 3 variants you write must test a different angle from `09_marketing_angles.md`, NOT a different desire. The desire stays the same across all 3.

## Output

Write **3 variants** (1200-1400 words each), one per top angle from step 9.

For each variant: label which angle it tests and which hook it opens with.
