# Marketing Ideas

139 proven strategies across 14 categories. Recommend 3-5 relevant ideas based on product stage and resources.

## Process

1. Check `output/{brand}/{product}/research/product-marketing-context.md` for product and audience context
2. Ask: current stage, biggest constraint (time/money/team), and one thing that's worked
3. Recommend 3-5 ideas with implementation steps
4. Prioritize by: impact × ease × fit for current stage

---

## Ideas by Stage

### Pre-Launch
- Waitlist + email nurture sequence
- Founder-led content (document the build)
- Beta user community (Discord, Slack)
- Product Hunt prep (relationships before launch day)
- Content SEO foundation (start 6 months early)

### Early Stage (0 → first 100 customers)
- Direct outreach to ICP (personal email, LinkedIn DM)
- Partner with complementary tools (joint newsletter, co-webinar)
- Forum presence (Reddit, Facebook groups, Slack communities)
- Founder PR (guest posts, podcast appearances)
- Referral program with meaningful incentive

### Growth Stage (100 → 1,000 customers)
- Paid ads (start with retargeting, then cold)
- Affiliate / influencer program
- SEO content machine (hire writer or use AI)
- Sales team + CRM
- Conference / event presence

### Scale (1,000+ customers)
- Brand campaigns (awareness, not just conversion)
- International expansion
- Acquisition of complementary products
- Enterprise sales motion
- Media/press relationships

## Ideas by Category

**Content & SEO**
- Comparison pages ("Product vs Competitor")
- Use-case landing pages ("[Product] for [role]")
- Free tools / calculators
- Original research / annual report
- YouTube tutorial series

**Paid Advertising**
- Meta: test 5 hooks × 3 angles × 2 creatives = 30 variants
- Google: branded + competitor + problem-aware keywords
- Retargeting: visit → trial → purchase sequence
- YouTube: 15-sec pre-roll (awareness) + VSL (conversion)
- Native ads (Taboola/Outbrain) for advertorial content

**Email**
- Welcome sequence (5 emails, 10 days)
- Re-engagement campaign for cold list
- Segmented campaigns by persona / use case
- Automated trial → paid conversion sequence

**Partnerships**
- Newsletter swaps (same audience, non-competing product)
- Integration partnerships (appear in their app directory)
- Co-created content (joint webinar, co-written guide)
- Reseller / white-label program

**Product-Led Growth**
- Free tier with viral mechanic ("Powered by X" badge)
- Shareable outputs (reports, certificates, results)
- Referral credit program
- Public roadmap (community involvement)

**Unconventional**
- Build in public (share revenue, failures, decisions)
- Direct competitor comparison ads
- Customer "roast" (invite honest criticism publicly)
- Give away a valuable tool for free (to build list)

## For Each Recommended Idea, Provide

1. **Name** — what to call it
2. **Why it fits** — connection to their specific product/audience
3. **First 3 steps** — concrete actions to start this week
4. **Expected outcome** — what success looks like in 30/90 days
5. **Resources needed** — time, money, people

## Self-Check

Before presenting output, verify:
- [ ] 3-5 ideas recommended — not the full category list
- [ ] Each idea is matched to the stated product stage (Pre-Launch / Early / Growth / Scale)
- [ ] Each idea includes all 5 required elements: Name, Why it fits, First 3 steps, Expected outcome, Resources needed
- [ ] Prioritization applied — ideas ranked by impact × ease × stage fit
- [ ] The user's biggest constraint (time / money / team) was asked and factored into recommendations

If any check fails → fix it before presenting.
