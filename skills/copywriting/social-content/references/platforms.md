# Platform-Specific Strategy Guide

Detailed strategies for each platform in a direct-response marketing context.

---

## Facebook

**Best for:** DR product promotion, community building, retargeting audiences, older demographics (35-65)
**Audience:** 35-65, purchase-intent, community-oriented
**Posting frequency:** 1-2x per day on pages; 3-5x per week in groups
**Best times:** 1-4pm weekdays; Thursday-Sunday for engagement

**What works:**
- Before/after transformation stories (image or video)
- Testimonial posts with real photos (not stock)
- Educational content that teases the mechanism ("why belly fat won't budge after 40")
- Native video (uploaded directly, not YouTube links)
- Facebook Groups for community + organic reach
- Live video (strongest reach boost)
- Polls that validate desires ("Which is your #1 struggle: energy, weight, or sleep?")

**What doesn't:**
- External links in post body (kills reach — put in comments)
- Hard sell in organic posts (saves it for ads)
- Generic stock photos
- Cross-posting from Instagram without adaptation
- Overly corporate tone

**Format tips:**
- Hook in first 2-3 lines (everything before "See More")
- Use line breaks — avoid walls of text
- Native video outperforms links 3-5x
- Carousel posts work for step-by-step content
- First comment = best place for the link

**Algorithm tips:**
- Comments > reactions > shares > clicks
- Replies to comments in first 60 minutes boost distribution
- Facebook prioritizes content that sparks conversation
- Groups content reaches members more reliably than pages
- Live video notifies followers in real time

## Instagram

**Best for:** Visual storytelling, transformation content, younger-to-mid demographics (25-50), product discovery
**Audience:** 25-50, visually-driven, aspirational, health/lifestyle oriented
**Posting frequency:** 1 feed post/day + 3-7 Stories/day + 1-2 Reels/week
**Best times:** 11am-1pm, 7-9pm; Tuesday-Friday

**What works:**
- Reels with transformation hooks (before/after reveal)
- Carousel "swipe to see" for educational content ("5 reasons your diet isn't working")
- Stories for testimonials, polls, and behind-the-scenes
- User-generated content (real customer photos/videos)
- Close-up product shots with benefit-focused captions
- "Save this" posts for tips (saves signal quality content)

**What doesn't:**
- Low-quality visuals
- Captions that start with the product name
- Too many hashtags in caption (move to first comment)
- Overly promotional Reels (they stop watch time)
- Ignoring Reels — it's the primary reach driver

**Format tips:**
- Reels: Hook in first 1-2 seconds is non-negotiable
- Caption: Lead with a hook line, expand in body, CTA at end
- Carousel: First slide = hook, last slide = CTA
- Stories: Use polls and questions to drive interaction
- Optimal caption length: 138-150 chars for discovery; longer for engaged audience

**Algorithm tips:**
- Reels get 2-3x reach of static posts
- Saves and shares matter more than likes
- Stories keep you top-of-feed for followers
- Watch time on Reels is the #1 signal
- Consistent posting beats sporadic high-quality posts

## TikTok

**Best for:** Viral reach, younger demographics (18-35), awareness-stage content, organic product discovery
**Audience:** 18-40, entertainment-first, health/wellness curious
**Posting frequency:** 1-4x per day
**Best times:** 7-9am, 12-3pm, 7-11pm

**What works:**
- Unpolished, authentic content (over-produced = brand account = less reach)
- Educational content in entertaining wrapper ("POV: you finally find out why your hair is thinning")
- Trending sounds with your content layered on top
- Responding to comments with video replies
- "Watch till end" hooks
- Transformation reveals
- Duets and stitches with relevant content

**What doesn't:**
- Horizontal video (vertical 9:16 only)
- Overly produced/polished content
- Ignoring trending sounds and formats
- Hard sell in first 3 seconds
- Repurposed Facebook/Instagram content without re-editing

**Format tips:**
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds — spoken AND visual
- Keep it under 30-45 seconds to start
- Text overlay for key points (many watch without sound)
- Strong verbal hook: "Stop doing X if you want Y"
- End with a pattern interrupt or cliffhanger to boost rewatch rate

**Video hook formulas for TikTok/Reels:**
```
"Stop [common behavior] if you [desire]"
"POV: You finally find out why [problem]"
"I [shocking result] in [timeframe] by doing this one thing"
"[Authority figure] told me [counterintuitive truth] about [problem]"
"Things no one tells you about [relatable problem]"
"Tell me you [relatable situation] without telling me"
```

**Algorithm tips:**
- Completion rate is the #1 signal — short videos that get rewatched outperform
- Shares outside TikTok boost reach significantly
- First 200-300 followers see your content (small test batch)
- Posting consistently trains the algorithm to your account
- Use 3-5 targeted hashtags, not 20+

## YouTube (Organic / Community)

**Best for:** Long-form education, product explanations, before/after case studies, search-driven discovery
**Audience:** All ages; health/wellness content skews 30-60
**Posting frequency:** 1-2x per week (Shorts: daily if possible)
**Best times:** Thursday-Saturday; 12pm-4pm

**What works:**
- Educational "why" videos (mechanism explanation)
- Testimonial compilations
- Product comparison/"does this work?" format
- YouTube Shorts for Reels-equivalent reach
- Long-form "story" videos (10-15 min) for warm audiences
- Q&A and comment response videos

**What doesn't:**
- Clickbait thumbnails that don't match content
- Videos without a clear search intent
- Skipping the hook in first 30 seconds

**Format tips:**
- Title = search intent first, curiosity second
- Thumbnail: face + emotion + benefit text
- Hook in first 30 seconds (re-state the promise)
- Chapters/timestamps improve watch time
- End screen with subscribe CTA

## Facebook Groups (Separate from Pages)

**Best for:** Community building, organic authority, social proof generation, list building
**Strategy:**

- Join groups where your avatar already is (not your own group)
- Provide genuine value before any promotion
- Answer questions using the mechanism (without naming product)
- Share relevant testimonials as "success stories I've seen"
- Soft CTA in bio/comments only

**Own group strategy:**
- Daily engagement posts (questions, polls)
- Weekly educational posts
- Member success story spotlight
- Live Q&As to drive attendance and sales
