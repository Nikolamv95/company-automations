# Post Format Templates

Ready-to-use templates for DR marketing brands across platforms.

---

## Facebook Post Templates

### The Transformation Story
```
[Hook: Specific result or moment — before "See More" cut]

[Set the scene: Who, what problem, how long they suffered]

[What they tried before — validate the struggle]

[The discovery / turning point]

[The result — specific, with numbers and timeframe]

[Bridge to reader: "If you're dealing with X, you're not alone."]

[Soft CTA — link in comments]
```

**Example:**
> She'd tried every diet since her 40s.
>
> Nothing worked.
>
> Keto, intermittent fasting, calorie counting — she did them all.
>
> Then her doctor mentioned something about cortisol she'd never heard before.
>
> 6 weeks later: 14 lbs gone. Without changing her diet.
>
> If you're struggling with stubborn belly fat after 40, the answer might not be what you think.
>
> Full story in the comments 👇

```
[Counterintuitive claim about their problem]

Here's what most people don't know:

[Fact 1 — specific, surprising]
[Fact 2 — validates their struggle]
[Fact 3 — teases the mechanism]

The real cause of [problem] isn't [what they think].

It's [mechanism].

[Comment or link CTA]
```

### The Testimonial Post
```
"[Verbatim quote from customer — specific result]"
— [First name, age, or descriptor]

[3-4 lines expanding the story: what they struggled with, what changed]

[Specific result with numbers]

[Bridge: "This is what's possible when..."]

[CTA]
```

## Instagram Caption Templates

```
[Hook line — mirrors the video hook, first 125 chars are visible]

[Takeaway or key insight]

[CTA: "Save this", "Follow for more", "Link in bio"]

[3-5 targeted hashtags — or in first comment]

```
[Hook — creates urgency to swipe]

Swipe to see [what they'll discover].

[1-2 lines of context]

[CTA: "Save this for later" / "Share with someone who needs this"]

## Instagram / Facebook Story Templates (3–5 Slides)

Stories disappear in 24h and reach existing followers. Use for warm-audience nurturing, testimonials, and soft CTAs.

### The Testimonial Story
```
Slide 1 [Hook]: "[First name] couldn't [desire] no matter what she tried."
               → Text overlay on plain background or product photo
Slide 2 [Problem]: "She'd tried [X], [Y], [Z]. Nothing worked. For [timeframe]."
Slide 3 [Turn]: "Then she discovered [mechanism/product category]."
               → Simple quote or result number
Slide 4 [Proof]: "[Specific result] in [timeframe]."
               → Before/after image or screenshot of review
Slide 5 [CTA]: "See her full story → [Link sticker]"
               → Or: "DM us 'story' for details"
```

### The Educational Story (3 slides)
```
Slide 1 [Question/Poll]: "Did you know [counterintuitive fact]?"
                         → Use Poll sticker: "Yes / No way"
Slide 2 [Reveal]: "Most people blame [X]. The real cause is [mechanism]."
Slide 3 [CTA]: "Tap the link to learn how to fix it →"
               → Link sticker or "Link in bio"
```

### The Behind-the-Scenes Story (4 slides)
```
Slide 1: "Here's how [product/ingredient] actually works 👇"
Slide 2: [Photo or short clip of product/process]
         "Most [products] do [X]. Ours does [Y] instead."
Slide 3: "[Specific differentiator or key ingredient]"
Slide 4: "Questions? DM us or tap below ↓" → Link sticker
```

**Story rules:**
- First slide must hook within 1 second — or they tap past
- Text overlay is mandatory — most people watch muted
- Use native Instagram/Facebook features (polls, questions, countdowns) — they boost reach
- CTA slide with link sticker converts better than "link in bio" redirect
- Post Stories daily — keeps account top-of-feed for followers

## TikTok / Reels Script Templates

### The Reveal Script
```
[0-2s] Hook (spoken): "[Shocking claim or problem statement]"
[2-5s] Setup: "Here's what nobody tells you about [problem]"
[5-20s] The insight: [Mechanism explained simply — 1 core point]
[20-28s] Proof: [Quick result or transformation reference]
[28-30s] CTA: "Follow for more on [topic]" or "Link in bio"
```

### The "3 Signs" Script
```
[0-2s] "3 signs that [problem] is actually [real cause]"
[2-8s] Sign 1: [Specific, relatable symptom]
[8-14s] Sign 2: [Specific, relatable symptom]
[14-20s] Sign 3: [Specific, relatable symptom]
[20-28s] "If you have all 3, [mechanism] might be why"
[28-30s] CTA
```

### The Myth Script
```
[0-2s] "[Common advice] is actually making your [problem] worse."
[2-8s] "Here's why:" — [Counterintuitive explanation]
[8-20s] "What actually works:" — [Solution/mechanism]
[20-28s] "I've seen [X people] use this and [result]"
[28-30s] CTA
```

## Hook Formulas by Platform

### Facebook (text-first, emotional)
```
Curiosity: "The real reason [common problem] happens after [age/event] isn't what you think."
Fear:      "If you're still [common behavior], you might be making [problem] worse."
Desire:    "[Specific result] in [timeframe] — without [common sacrifice]."
Story:     "She hadn't worn [item of clothing] in [time]. Then [discovery]."
Proof:     "[X people] have [achieved result] using [mechanism]. Here's how."
```

### Instagram (visual-first, aspirational)
```
Curiosity: "Swipe to see what [X people] discovered about [problem] ➡️"
Desire:    "[Result] is possible — even if you've [failed before]. Here's proof."
Story:     "She gave up after [time] of trying. Then this happened. 👇"
Identity:  "For women who refuse to accept that [problem] is 'just part of aging'"
```

### TikTok/Reels (spoken hook, pattern interrupt)
```
"Stop [common behavior] if you want [desire]"
"POV: you find out the real reason [problem] won't go away"
"I [achieved result] in [timeframe] doing this — and I was skeptical"
"[Authority] told me something about [problem] that changed everything"
"Things no one tells you about [relatable problem] after [age/event]"
"Wait until the end — this is the part that surprised me"
```

## Content Pillar Distribution (DR Brand)

For a health/wellness DTC product:

```
30% — Educational (mechanism, "why" content, myth-busting)
25% — Social proof (testimonials, before/after, customer stories)
20% — Entertainment / relatability (problem humor, "this is me" content)
15% — Behind the scenes (product, brand, process)
10% — Direct offer (promotions, launches, limited offers)
```

**Rule:** 90% value/story/proof → 10% direct offer in organic. Ads handle conversion.
