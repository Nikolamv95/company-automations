## What Makes These Work

- **Specificity**: "56 years old", "$5,000 deposit", "6 months", "ziplock bag" — concrete details create credibility
- **Conflict**: A trusted authority (doctor, vet) says something unexpected
- **Stakes**: Something precious is at risk (appearance, hair, pet's life)
- **Pattern interrupt**: CAPSLOCK used once for genuine shock, not decoration
- **No product name**: The hook is about the problem/discovery, not the solution
