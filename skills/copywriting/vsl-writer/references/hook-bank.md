# Video Ad Hook Bank

Hooks organized by platform, awareness level, and emotional trigger. The hook is the first 1–5 seconds — it determines if anyone watches the rest.

---

## Hook Principles

**Facebook / Instagram (feed):** Autoplay, muted. Visual hook must carry meaning before audio loads.
- Text overlay is the real hook
- Spoken hook reinforces
- Pattern interrupt visual stops the scroll

**TikTok:** Sound-on culture but thumb-first. Spoken hook must land in first 1 second.
- Informal, native tone — not polished
- "You" language — direct address

**YouTube pre-roll:** Sound on. Viewer is watching another video — you're an interruption.
- Must hook before 5-second skip button
- Address them directly: "If you're [X]..."

## Hook Formulas by Emotional Trigger

### Curiosity Hooks
```
"The real reason [common problem] won't go away isn't what you think."
"What your doctor won't tell you about [problem]."
"I found out something about [problem] that made me furious."
"There's a reason [frustrating outcome] keeps happening — and it has nothing to do with [assumed cause]."
"Nobody talks about this part of [problem]."
```

### Fear / Pattern Interrupt Hooks
```
"STOP [common behavior] if you [desire]."
"Warning: [common advice] might be making your [problem] worse."
"If you're over [age] and [problem] won't move — listen to this."
"This is the part where most [solution] fail — and why."
"[Specific behavior] is secretly sabotaging your [goal]."
```

### Desire / Result Hooks
```
"[Name] lost [specific result] in [timeframe] — without [common sacrifice]."
"What if [desired outcome] had nothing to do with [painful thing they've been doing]?"
"[Number] women over [age] did this and [specific result]. Here's what they found."
"[Result] is possible — even if you've tried [X], [Y], and [Z]."
"This one thing helped [X people] [result] — when nothing else worked."
```

### Identity / Avatar Call-Out Hooks
```
"For women over 45 who feel like their body is working against them."
"If you've tried every diet and nothing sticks — this is for you."
"This is for anyone who's done everything right and still can't [desired outcome]."
"[Avatar descriptor] — you need to see this."
"To everyone who's been told [problem] is just part of aging: it's not."
```

### Contrarian / Myth-Bust Hooks
```
"Unpopular opinion: [common advice] is the reason you're not getting results."
"Everything you've been told about [problem] is wrong."
"[Counterintuitive truth about their problem]."
"Eating less is making your belly fat worse. Here's why."
"Cardio doesn't fix this. Here's what does."
```

### Social Proof Hooks
```
"[Number] women over [age] used this to [result] in [timeframe]."
"Before I show you the results — let me explain what happened."
"She hadn't [desired action] in [timeframe]. Then she tried this."
"[Number] reviews. [Average result]. One common ingredient."
```

## Hooks by Platform

### Facebook Cold Traffic (30s format)
Needs: scroll-stop visual + immediate identification
```
"If you're over 45 and belly fat won't budge — this is why."
"She lost 23 lbs in 6 weeks. She hadn't changed her diet."
"Stop blaming willpower. Your hormones changed."
"The real reason diets stop working after 45 isn't what you think."
"[TEXT OVERLAY: "It's not food. It's cortisol."]"
```

### Facebook Retargeting (60s format)
Needs: reference to previous awareness, proof-forward
```
"You've probably seen this before — but have you seen the results?"
"Linda tried this 6 weeks ago. Here's what happened."
"Still thinking about it? Here's what 4,200 women found out."
"The guarantee means zero risk. Here's why that matters."
```

### TikTok In-Feed (15–30s format)
Needs: casual, native, immediate — no setup
```
"POV: you find out your belly fat has nothing to do with calories."
"Okay this is embarrassing to admit but [personal admission]..."
"I tested [X] for 30 days. Here's what actually happened."
"3 signs your [problem] is actually a [real cause] problem:"
"Things nobody tells you about [problem] after [age/event]."
"Tell me you've [relatable situation] without telling me."
```

### YouTube Pre-Roll (must survive 5-second skip)
Needs: direct address, urgency, specific audience call-out — all in first 5 seconds
```
[0-5s, works standalone if viewer skips immediately:]
"If you're over 45 and belly fat won't move — don't skip this."
"Before you skip — this is why your diet stopped working after 40."
"Skip if you want. But this is the one thing that finally worked for [X] women."
"What I'm about to show you takes 30 seconds. And it might change everything."
```

### Instagram Reels Ad (15–30s format)
Needs: strong first frame, text overlay-led, aspirational tone
```
"She wore a size 6 for the first time in 8 years."
"From [before state] to [after state] — no extreme dieting."
"The [mechanism] that finally made the difference."
"This is what changing one thing looks like over [timeframe]."
```

## YouTube Ad Formats — Hook Strategy

### 6-Second Bumper (no skip — one shot)
Goal: brand recall or single-message impact. Cannot do story.
```
Strategy A — Result: "[Result] in [timeframe]. [Brand name]."
Strategy B — Problem: "Belly fat after 45? [Brand name] addresses the real cause."
Strategy C — Disruption: "Most diets fail after 45. [Brand name] was built for what happens next."
```

### 15s Non-Skippable
Goal: awareness + intrigue. Must deliver a complete thought.
```
[0-5s]  Hook + avatar call-out
[5-12s] One insight or mechanism tease
[12-15s] CTA or brand
```

### 30s Skippable — The Split Rule
The first 5 seconds must earn the skip button.
The next 25 seconds must earn the click.

```
First 5s goal: Make viewer want to see where this goes
Next 25s goal: Deliver enough proof that clicking feels worth it
```

Best performing structures for DR/supplement:
1. Problem story → mechanism tease → proof stat → CTA
2. Myth bust → real cause → result claim → CTA
3. Avatar call-out → validation → discovery → soft CTA

## Awareness Level Hook Guide

| Awareness | Hook approach | Example |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| 1 (Unaware) | Problem-first. Name their pain before anything else. | "Why does belly fat get harder to lose after 45?" |
| 2 (Problem aware) | Agitate the problem. Show you understand it deeply. | "You've tried everything. Here's the reason nothing worked." |
| 3 (Solution aware) | Differentiate. Why is this different from what they've seen? | "You've probably tried [category]. Here's what they all missed." |
| 4 (Product aware) | Proof and conviction. They know you — give them a reason to act now. | "[Number] women. [Average result]. Here's the one they picked." |
| 5 (Most aware) | Offer-forward. They just need the push. | "Still thinking about it? Here's the guarantee." |

## Hook Testing Protocol

Always launch 3 hook variants on cold traffic (A/B/C test):

```
Hook A: Curiosity / mechanism angle
Hook B: Result / social proof angle
Hook C: Identity / avatar call-out angle

Same body copy after the hook.
Evaluate by: Thumbstop rate (3s view %), Video completion rate, CTR.

Winner: highest combination of completion + CTR (not just thumbstop).
```
